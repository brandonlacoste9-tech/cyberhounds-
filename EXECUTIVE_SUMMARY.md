# 🐺 Cyberhound B2B
## Executive Summary

**Cyberhound** is an autonomous compliance gap hunting platform that discovers $5K-25K B2B deals in real-time.

---

## The Problem

Enterprises expanding globally face **regulatory complexity**:
- **GDPR** (Europe): €20M fines for non-compliance
- **CCPA/CPRA** (California): $7,500 per violation
- **Law 25** (Quebec): Up to $100,000 penalties
- **Bill 96** (Quebec): Mandatory AI disclosure

**Manual compliance monitoring is:**
- Slow (legal teams take weeks)
- Expensive ($500+/hour for compliance consultants)
- Reactive (find out after the fine)

---

## The Solution

Cyberhound deploys **20 autonomous scouts** that:
1. **Hunt** - Scan target websites 24/7 for compliance gaps
2. **Analyze** - Detect missing DPOs, undisclosed AI, privacy violations
3. **Price** - Calculate deal value based on fine risk
4. **Alert** - Send Decision Packs via Telegram for approval
5. **Strike** - Deploy solutions through contractor network

**Human-on-the-Loop (HOTL)**: Executives tap ✅ to approve strikes from their phone.
